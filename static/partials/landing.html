What is Liftoff?

Liftoff is changing the way events, concerts or meet ups are funded. Support your favorite artist/event/charity, or see how you can pre-sell the demand for your own endeavors!

Who is Liftoff?

Liftoff is a group funding platform to help an event organizer reduce their Risk and upfront costs. Whether it's tickets for a concert, a convention, or a comedy show, Liftoff is the simplest and most efficient way to test the demand for your event.

What's Liftoff cost?

Our service is completely free for event attendees. They only see a final cost, not crazy Fees added on. We keep the lights on by charging a 5% fee to the event organizer.
