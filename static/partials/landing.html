What is Liftoff?

Liftoff is changing the way tickets are bought and sold. Support your favorite artist/event, or see how you can leverage pre-selling for your own endeavours!

Who is Liftoff for?

Liftoff is a platform for anyone who needs to sell tickets. Whether it's tickets for a concert, a convention, or a comedy show, Liftoff is the simplest and most efficient way to distribute tickets.

Is it free?

Our service is completely free for buyers. Creating an event is free. We charge a flat 5% of sales for ticket sales with no other fees.
